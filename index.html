📋 How to set this up:
1. Go to emailjs.com and create a free account
2. Click "Email Services" → connect your Gmail
3. Click "Email Templates" → create a template (use variables: {{member_name}}, {{risk_score}}, {{agency}})
4. Go to "Account" → copy your Public Key
5. Paste all three IDs above and click Save
Then whenever you click "Email Drew Now" on the Membership tab, it sends automatically!
